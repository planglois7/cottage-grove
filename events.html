June 24- Cottage Grove Carnival

Come one, come all to our Carousel Kickoff Event! Cottage Grove is a tightknit community just south of Eugene with hopes and dreams of building a carousel on our Main Street. To raise funds, we will be hosting a full day of food and fun in our town on June 24th. We would love for you to come and be a part of this special moment!

July 29th- Carousel Color Run

Learn to paint like pro while sipping some pinot. Join us for our paint and sip fundraiser event on July 8th in Portland, OR. The proceeds will help continue to raise money for the Cottage Grove carousel, a fundamental piece of the Cottage Grove community. Tickets are $40 per person, which includes a three-hour painting lesson, wine, and supplies.

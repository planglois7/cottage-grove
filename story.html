Our Story

30 unique animals found their way to Cottage Grove in the mid-90s. Individually shipped from Virginia, they traveled 2000 miles west with one goal in mind: bring people together.

After successfully relocating, numerous complications have made it difficult for this gorgeous carousel to shine from the barn it's been stored in for more than 20 years.

We need your help to reignite the light.

This carousel will be a catalyst for coming together; a gathering spot for all walks of life to enjoy the magic of the present moment. Community-minded volunteers and businesses are now donating time and materials to refurbish the animals and the mechanism.

We need your financial support now to create a home for it once the restoration work is completed to bring it back to life for the enjoyment of generations to come.

By supporting the Cottage Grove Carousel, your name will be carved in our town's history.
